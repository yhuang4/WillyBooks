How to Sell

1. Get an account!
2. Click "Sell"
3. Enter all information requested
4. Don't forget to take pictures of your book
5. Decide the price and click "Submit"
6. If someone wants your book, they will contact you, and you guys can meet up and exchange money and books
7. After a transaction, go to "My Account" and delete the book so you won't be bothered by future buyers!

How to Buy

1. Click "Buy"
2. Search by ISBN or view all books
3. You'll see books for sale posted by people
4. If you like a book, contact the seller and schedule a meeting where you guys can exchange moeny and books
